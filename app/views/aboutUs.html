About TMC

Thornwell Maintenance Corporation is the success story of a classic American tale of hard work and perseverance starting in 1987. We are the complete cleaning solution: building maintenance, janitorial services, and food sanitation for industrial plants. The dedication and personalized attention to detail is the reason why clients have continued to use our services for the last 29 years.
